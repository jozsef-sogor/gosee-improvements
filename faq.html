FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

We are constantly trying to deliver the best service and this is why is important to make it clear for everyone what GoSEE is all about: making traveling simple, efficient and fun.

If you have any further questions for us, we are glad to get in contact - just email us at contact@gosee.com

For users

  • Why should I use GoSee?
  • GoSEE is addressed to all of us who are tired of dull and sinuous trip organizing. If you think traveling should be an easy-to-plan experience and a fun-to-enjoy game, then GoSee is for you.
  • Is GoSee free?
  • Anyone can enjoy GoSee by creating a free account. This grants access to most of the app features.
    If you want to enjoy premium features (e.g. limited badges granting you special discounts or boosted planning features), you can choose one of the Premium accounts.
  • How do I manage my account?
  • Once you install the app, you can create and fully customize your profile account by accessing the Profile module.
    This is where you can also delete your account from. (Although this is sad to think of, isnâ€™t it?)
  • How does the game work?
  • The idea is very simple: the more you use GoSee for planning your trips and for mapping the places you visit, the bigger the discounts you can win through the badges you have on your account.
    Once in a while, we launch challenges where users can win rare and precious badges - these will guarantee access to temporary special discounts.
  • What happens to my personal info after I submit it?
  • GoSee will only use the provided data for the intended & declared purpose stated in the collecting form. We do not sell or give any of the data collected on the website or within the app.
  • How can I benefit from becoming a business partner?
  • Our partners make an important pillar of our travel community and we strive to reward them accordingly. If interested in the way we can increase your visibility by becoming our partner, you are welcome to email us on contact@gosee.com
